Title: Triple-Lock Safety Protocol (2025)
Details: This protocol requires three layers of verification for any AI-generated action. 
1. The Maker generates the response.
2. The Checker validates the response against the context.
3. The Sanitizer removes any PII (Personally Identifiable Information).

Title: Agentic Hallucination Prevention
Details: To prevent hallucinations, agents must use 'Grounding', where they are strictly forbidden from answering using internal training data if external context is available.
